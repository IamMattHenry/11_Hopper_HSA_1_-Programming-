ABM (Accountancy, Business & Management)

ABM or Accountancy, Business & Management strand would focus on the basic concepts of financial management, business management, corporate operations, and all things that are accounted for.

ABM subjects will equip you with skills in statistics, economics, and accounting fundamentals, and also prepare you for your college classes with baseline knowledge on management, marketing, and business ethics.
